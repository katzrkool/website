Auto DND

Depending on time of day, this shortcut will set your phone's Do Not Disturb until the next major break in the day. If it's before lunch, it wil go into DND until 12:00 or 11:55, depending on the day, and then after lunch, once triggered, go into dnd until 15:30
If it's the weekend or school is over, the shortcut will launch DND Menu if installed.

DND Menu

This shortcut presents a menu of times, (ex: 5 mins, 15 mins, 2 hrs, etc), and sets DND for that long
