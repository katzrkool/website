Trips

A free and open source packing list manager for iOS. Keep track of items on trips as big as month-long vacations or as short as a sleepover.

Multiple Trips

Each with their own color and icon!

Categories

Keep your Trips organized with categories, to group similar items.

Templates

Build a category once, and add it to a Trip with a couple taps.

Dark Mode

Supports iOS Dark Mode (and light mode as well).

iPad Support

Support for iOS devices of all sizes and shapes.

Additional Features

  • iCloud Sync
  • Icon/Color Selection
  • Support for multiple legs of a Trip
  • Generate Templates from existing Categories
  • Create items in bulk
  • Written in native iOS frameworks (SwiftUI!). No cross-platform JS stuff.
  • And much more!
