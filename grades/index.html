The New School Grade Checker has been shutdown

Summer has begun, and so the Grade Checker has been shutdown. If you still want to see your grades, you can go to the myBackpack portal to check your final grades.

Unfortunately, since I won't be attending The New School, it is no longer possible for me to maintain the Grade Checker for the coming year. If you are interested in continuing the project, check out the Github Repo, and feel free to fork it, or just clone your own copy to run. Thanks to everyone who tried/used the application, it means a lot.
